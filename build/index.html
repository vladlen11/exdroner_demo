Exdroner can provide full-use of avaliable agricultural land by cheap and effective spraying from the air. Farmers will have convinient and simple mean of spraying

Widespread emergency vehicle

Cheap and affordable solution for emergency situations.

Emergency services can use helicopters for evacuating people and Exdroner for monitoring, equipment delivery. Exdroner can provide emergency services with quick deploying, fast and reliable vehicle. Exdroner can work with minimum infrastructure far from civilization.

Chance to survive

Timely, reliable and cheap delivery of human organs and biomaterials.

Heart for transplantation has no right to stuck in traffic jam. It has no right to double the distance on a mountainous road. But its transportation has to be cost-effective and accurate. Exdroner can provide you with all of this

excellence of drone

Long range
of flight

Exdroner can fly up to 190 miles away with maximum payload. And even more with some improvements

Vertical take-off
and landing

Take-off like multicopter, then flying like a plane and landing vertical. All this is possible with Exdroned. Moreover, you can hover above the object for loading/unloading

9 kg
payload

Full set of cameras, spraying tank, cargobox - you choose what you will transport. High flexibility gives a customer an opportunity to use it for different applications depending on situation

FAR Part 107
applicable

The MTOW of Exdroner is applicable for usage with no waivers of FAR Part 107. 55 pounds is normal take-off weight for Exdroner. The frame can take even more but you should change the motors.

Cruise speed
60-80 mph

High speed can allow you to deliver your cargo fast. You can also fly in low-speed mode of 25 mph for spraying or territory monitoring.

No need of
charging

You can deploy Exdroner far from civilization with minimum additional materials. Just fuel and laptop, tablet or smartphone. Exdroner will take care of the rest.

What is the benefit of exdroner

  • Over 600 million dollars of potential demand only on the USA territory.
    Farmers, rescue and air medical services together make a great market opportunity for project success. Our job is to give them possibilities.
  • High product margin
    High-tech industries always have high margin. But we are able to reduce production costs to minimum providing good profit and possibilities for growth and development.
  • Relatively simple and quick process of organizing production
    Designing Exdroner we tried to make all production operations quick and simple. We refused some ultramodern solutions to reduce the labor input and need of expensive equipment. A lot of production work can be made by contractors.
